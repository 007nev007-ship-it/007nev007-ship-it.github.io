Research basis

The research behind BridgeEDU

BridgeEDU is grounded in postgraduate research on ICT-mediated intercultural communication in education. The purpose of this page is to present that foundation in clear, accessible language.

Meta-analysis

The research included a meta-analysis of four studies related to ICT-enhanced communication and intercultural communication in educational settings. Taken together, the studies suggested a meaningful relationship between communication-related factors and educational interaction, while also showing notable variation across contexts.

Survey strand

The broader study also included survey data from foreign English language teachers in Thai educational settings. This part of the research explored perceived communication effectiveness, ICT use, and communication-related factors in everyday school interaction.

Practical implications

The research suggests that communication effectiveness can be improved through clearer digital practices, more thoughtful message design, and better awareness of intercultural and institutional expectations.

In other words, small changes in wording, timing, tone, and communication structure can make a real difference.
