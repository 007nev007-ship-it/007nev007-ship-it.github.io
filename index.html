From thesis to specialised platform

The core purpose of BridgeEDU is to convert academic research into usable support for real schools. Rather than leaving the thesis as a static document, this website gives it a practical public-facing form.

The research basis

Your thesis gives this website a credible foundation. That should be visible. The research section helps establish authority while still remaining readable for a general audience.

Meta-analysis

A four-study meta-analysis examined ICT-enhanced communication and intercultural communication barriers within relevant educational contexts. The results suggested a meaningful relationship, while also showing strong heterogeneity across studies.

Survey strand

The survey explored how foreign English language teachers perceived communication effectiveness, ICT use, and communication-related factors in Thai educational settings.

Practical implication

Taken together, the findings support the idea that communication quality can be improved through clear digital practices, appropriate wording, and more thoughtful intercultural awareness.
